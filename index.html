New to GEN2? Three things to know

  1. Cases are the frame. Every spot you fill in the grid is a case — cases QuickLock together into one structure.
  2. You choose what goes in each case — a Classic or Decor drawer, a shelf, or a cabinet. Swap them anytime.
  3. The mount holds it all — rails under a table, a Table Top Kit on a surface, or wall mounts.
